// The Formation Mechanism

Container Protocol

1. The PoW Container (HACD)

Users must commit an existing, scarce HACD unit (Proof-of-Work Diamond) as the cryptographic vault. The diamond acts as a scarce raw material container, ensuring the protocol borrows real computational history.

Input requirement: 1 HACD / Lot
Capital expenditure

2. The Energy Input (HAC)

A stack cost of 50 HAC must be committed per lot. This acts as a permanent protocol capital input. Committing HAC binds economic value directly to the asset, creating an unforgeable production cost.

Burn parameters: Sink Accumulation
Asset Extraction

3. The Asset State (CHRONO)

21,000 CHRONO are extracted and formed per lot, permanently binding the historical genesis metadata of the underlying HACD unit to the blockchain ledger. Once written, the relationship is cryptographic and immutable.

Mint outcome: 21,000 Shards / Lot
